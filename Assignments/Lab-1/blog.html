Just some things about me:

My name is Francisco Gonzalez, a dedicated student enrolled in the Computer Programming program at Durham College. Amidst the rigors of academia, I find peace in the companionship of my three cats and dog, whose presence adds warmth to my days. Beyond the realm of technology, I indulge in the thrill of motorcycle rides, relishing the sense of freedom they offer on the open road. Sports, particularly hockey and volleyball, serve as outlets for both physical activity and friendly competition, providing a welcomed balance to my academic pursuits. Additionally, I'm an avid gamer, finding enjoyment in immersive RPGs and strategic online battles. My commitment extends beyond personal interests; I hold dual roles as a valued team member at Costco and a dedicated skating instructor for the City of Oshawa. It's a busy yet fulfilling life, characterized by a blend of academic excellence, personal passions, and meaningful community involvement.
